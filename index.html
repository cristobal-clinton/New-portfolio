Data Annotation — Entity Recognition

AI Training

Raw Text Input

"Dr. Sarah Chen from Stanford Medical Center reported that the new drug Metformin-X showed 45% efficacy in phase 3 trials conducted in Boston on March 15, 2024."

→ Unstructured, no entity labels

Annotated Output

[PERSON] Dr. Sarah Chen from [ORG] Stanford Medical Center reported that the new drug [MEDICATION] Metformin-X showed [PERCENT] 45% efficacy in [TRIAL_PHASE] phase 3 trials conducted in [LOC] Boston on [DATE] March 15, 2024.

→ Fully annotated with entity labels
